Meet the No Sleep Beverage Team

Nick Papanicolaou
Founder & CEO

Nick Papanicolaou is a seasoned finance, operations, and startup professional with over 20 years of experience in managing deals, valuing companies, forecasting trends, launching ventures, improving efficiencies, and scaling businesses....He has spent the last 13 years in the alcohol beverage industry, where he notably launched his own brand and headed the M&A team for Pernod Ricard USA before launching New Brand Ventures for Pernod Ricard. Most recently, Nick served as the CEO of Castle Brands, a $40M spirits portfolio. Throughout his career, Nick has deployed over $700M into small and emerging alcohol beverage brands. Before entering the alcohol beverage industry, Nick spent 6 years in investment banking. He is a graduate of Columbia Business School, where he received his MBA in Value Investing and Entrepreneurship, and a graduate of Harvard College. Nick's passion lies in the intersection of beverages, culture, and community. Nick can typically be found sipping on Sotol Palomas in Zihuatanejo, Mexico.

He is a seasoned corporate development and M&A professional and also serves as an investor, board advisor, ... and fractional CFO for various emerging CPG brands. Prior to his entrepreneurial ventures, David worked as an investment banker at Deutsche Bank and Nomura. Marketing Advisor
